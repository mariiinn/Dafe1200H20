| Challenge

We were looking to solve the challenge of children not returning to school, either because of covid 19 or for other reasons. The first part of the problem is to find out why so many children are not returning to school. The second part of the problem is to do something about it when we know these reasons.

| Solution

We have choosen Uganda as a characteristic country where the soulution will work. (There is 65% mobile coverage in Uganda ).

Introducing STAY, an app used by teachers for attendance registration.

The phones are distributed to the teachers so that as many students as possible can be covered without the need for their own mobile device. The app will have a photo of every student in the class. If you click on a picture of a student, you will be sent to another page where the teacher can note attendance or select the type of absence. The options will be "Covid", "Health", "Work", "Family" and "Other reasos".

The data obtained from STAY will be used to make a model that shows how the different variables affects eachother. In other words make a virtual presentation of the world with focus upon absence. Uganda Bureau of Statistics (UBOS) will have access to anonymous/encrypted information obtained by STAY. The cooperation with UBOS will lead to more effective measures from the various municipalities that receive the statistics.
